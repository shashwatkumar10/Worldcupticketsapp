## ğŸ¯ Tournament Format

### Group Stage (June 11-27)

- **12 groups** of 4 teams each
- Each team plays **3 matches** (round-robin)
- **Top 2 teams** from each group advance (24 teams)
- **Best 8 third-place teams** also advance
- Total advancing to knockout: **32 teams**

### Knockout Stage Format

```
Round of 32 (16 matches)
    â†“
Round of 16 (8 matches)
    â†“
Quarterfinals (4 matches)
    â†“

    â†“

```

### Third-Place Qualification Rules

The 8 best third-placed teams are determined by:
1. Points
2. Goal difference
3. Goals scored
4. Fair play points
5. FIFA ranking

### Tie-Breaking Procedures

**Group Stage:**
1. Points
2. Goal difference
3. Goals scored
4. Head-to-head points
5. Head-to-head goal difference
6. Head-to-head goals scored
7. Fair play points
8. Drawing of lots

**Knockout Stage:**
- Extra time (2 x 15 minutes)
- Penalty shootout

## â“ FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: When does the 2026 World Cup start and end?**
A: June 11 - July 19, 2026 (39 days)

**Q: How many teams will participate?**
A: 48 teams total. Currently 42 teams have qualified, with 6 remaining spots to be decided via playoffs in March 2026 (4 UEFA playoffs + 2 Intercontinental playoffs).

**Q: How many matches will be played?**
A: 104 matches total

**Q: Which stadium hosts the final?**
A: MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey

**Q: Who are the defending champions?**
A: Argentina (won 2022 World Cup in Qatar)

### Tickets & Attendance

**Q: How can I get tickets?**
A: Through FIFA.com's official ticket portal. Pre-sales occurred in 2024-2025, with general sales in early 2026.

**Q: What are ticket prices?**
A: Range from approximately $50 (Category 4 group stage) to $2,000+ (Category 1 Final)

**Q: Can I buy tickets from resellers?**
A: Only use FIFA's official resale platform. Third-party resellers carry high fraud risk.

### Travel

**Q: Do I need a visa?**
A: Depends on nationality. Most European, Australian, and Japanese visitors need ESTA (USA), eTA (Canada). Check specific requirements.

**Q: What's the best city to base yourself?**
A: Depends on which games you want to see. Multi-city trips are common. New York/New Jersey offers most knockout matches.

**Q: How expensive are hotels during the World Cup?**
A: Expect 2-3x normal rates. Book early!

### Format & Rules

**Q: How does the new format work?**
A: 12 groups of 4 teams. Top 2 from each group (24 teams) plus best 8 third-place teams (32 total) advance to Round of 32.

**Q: Are there still penalty shootouts?**
A: Yes, in knockout rounds after extra time if needed.

**Q: What time zone is the final?**
A: Eastern Time (ET). 3:00 PM local, 8:00 PM UK, 9:00 PM Central Europe.
